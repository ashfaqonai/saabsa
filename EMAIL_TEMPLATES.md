## Tips for Responding:

1. **Respond within 24 hours** - Quick response shows professionalism
2. **Personalize** - Mention something specific if you can
3. **Be clear about next steps** - Make it easy for them to take action
4. **Highlight value** - Focus on benefits, not just features
5. **Include social proof** - Mention your track record (150+ projects, etc.)
6. **Make it easy to schedule** - Offer specific times or a calendar link
7. **Follow up** - If no response in 3-5 days, send a brief follow-up

## What to Include in Your Response:

✓ Thank them for their interest
✓ Brief overview of Saabsa Solutions
✓ Key differentiators (healthcare-focused, AI expertise, etc.)
✓ Clear next steps
✓ Multiple ways to connect
✓ Professional but friendly tone
