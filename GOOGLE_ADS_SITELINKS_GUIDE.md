## üìù How to Fill Out the Sitelinks Form

### For Each Sitelink:

1. **Sitelink text** (Required, max 25 characters):
   - Short, clear link text
   - Example: `Services`, `Patientree AI`, `Blog`

2. **Description line 1** (Recommended, max 35 characters):
   - What users will find on that page
   - Example: `Custom Software Development & AI Solutions`

3. **Description line 2** (Recommended, max 35 characters):
   - Additional benefit or detail
   - Example: `Healthcare Technology Consulting`

4. **Final URL** (Required):
   - Exact page URL on your website
   - Must be HTTPS
   - Example: `https://www.saabsa.com/services.html`

## üöÄ How to Add Sitelinks in Google Ads

### Step 1: In the Modal
1. Click **"+ Add"** or fill in the sitelink fields
2. Start with **Sitelink 1**

### Step 2: Fill Each Sitelink
1. **Sitelink text**: Enter short text (e.g., "Services")
2. **Description line 1**: Enter first description
3. **Description line 2**: Enter second description (optional but recommended)
4. **Final URL**: Enter full URL (e.g., `https://www.saabsa.com/services.html`)

### Step 3: Add More Sitelinks
1. Scroll down to **Sitelink 2**
2. Repeat the process
3. Add 4-6 sitelinks total (Google shows up to 4-6 in search results)

### Step 4: Preview
- Check the **Ad preview** on the right
- Verify sitelinks look good
- Make sure URLs are correct

### Step 5: Apply
1. Click **"Apply"** button
2. Google will review and approve
3. Sitelinks will start showing in your ads
