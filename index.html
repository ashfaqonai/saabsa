Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Saabsa Solutions and our services

What makes Saabsa Solutions different from other software development companies?

Saabsa Solutions specializes exclusively in healthcare technology. We combine deep AI expertise with healthcare industry knowledge to deliver solutions that understand the unique challenges of healthcare organizations. Our flagship product, Patientree AI, demonstrates our ability to build production-ready healthcare automation platforms.

Do you work with existing EHR systems?

Yes! We specialize in EHR integrations, including AthenaHealth, Epic, Cerner, and other major systems. Our Patientree AI platform includes seamless EHR integration capabilities, and we can build custom integrations for your specific needs.

How do you ensure HIPAA compliance?

HIPAA compliance is built into every solution we develop. We follow industry best practices for data encryption, access controls, audit logging, and business associate agreements. Our cloud infrastructure is configured to meet healthcare compliance requirements from day one.

What is your development process?

We use an agile, AI-enhanced development methodology that accelerates delivery while maintaining quality. We start with discovery and strategy, then move through iterative development with regular client feedback. Our AI-powered tools help us deliver faster without compromising on quality or security.

Can you help with both new development and existing system enhancements?

Absolutely. We build new applications from scratch, enhance existing systems, and provide ongoing maintenance and support. Whether you need a complete platform like Patientree AI or enhancements to your current EHR system, we can help.

What kind of support do you provide after launch?

We offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, feature enhancements, security updates, and 24/7 monitoring options. Our support packages are tailored to your needs, from basic maintenance to full managed services.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple integrations might take 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive platforms like Patientree AI take several months. Our AI-enhanced development process typically reduces timelines by 30-40% compared to traditional methods. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you offer consulting services for technology strategy?

Yes! We provide strategic technology consulting to help healthcare organizations develop IT roadmaps, evaluate technology options, and prioritize initiatives. Our consulting services help ensure your technology investments align with business objectives and deliver measurable ROI.
